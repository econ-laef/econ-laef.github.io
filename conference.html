Demographic Inequality in the Macroeconomy

The University at Albany, SUNY, the Laboratory for Aggregate Economics and Finance at University of California, Santa Barbara, and the Program on Economic Inclusion at the Federal Reserve Bank of Cleveland, invite submissions for a conference on demographic inequality and the macroeconomy to be held in Albany, NY, on April 11th and 12th, 2025.

The focus of the conference is group-level inequality, such as differences in outcomes by race, gender, or ethnicity, but papers addressing related issues either empirically or theoretically are also encouraged. Preference will be given to papers with a macroeconomic focus that have not previously been presented at major conferences.
